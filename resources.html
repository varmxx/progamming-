Resources for Advanced Programmers

Books and Advanced Programming Guides

1. "Clean Code: A Handbook of Agile Software Craftsmanship" by Robert C. Martin
Classic guide that teaches you principles for writing clean, maintainable code.
2. "Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software" by Erich Gamma, Richard Helm, Ralph Johnson, and John Vlissides
An essential book on design patterns that every advanced programmer should read.
3. "Effective Java" by Joshua Bloch
Offers advanced tips and best practices for Java development.
4. "Introduction to the Theory of Computation" by Michael Sipser
An in-depth book on the theory of computation and automata theory.
